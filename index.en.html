About Diving Certification

Benefits of Getting Certified

  • Dive at diving spots worldwide
  • Often receive discounts on equipment rental
  • Dive with friends without an instructor
  • Dive at greater depths
  • Enjoy various diving styles as you improve

Steps to Certification

  1. 1

    Application & Materials

    Receive study materials for pre-learning

  2. 2

    Theory Course

    Learn basic diving knowledge

  3. 3

    Pool Training

    Practice basic skills in shallow pool

  4. 4

    Ocean Training

    Complete 4 dives in the ocean

  5. 5

    Certification & Card

    Receive internationally recognized C-card
